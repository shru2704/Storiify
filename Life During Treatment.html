Life During Treatment

Patients about to undergo treatment for an illness often feel overwhelmed with how much they need to learn. Questions abound. What is the treatment process? How am I going to feel? How can I cover the expenses? The goal for this page is to answer those questions and many more. It’s a resource which you can access anytime, anywhere. We encourage you to use it and feel free to share your feedback.
